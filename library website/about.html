ABOUT

history

The school library was established in 1985 to provide students
with access to a wide range of books and learning resources.
It was established to support the academic needs of students
and teachers.
To support academic excellence and encourage independent
learning among students

The building itself was used by the Akamba to carry out their
cultural activities in the 1950s: thus the nickname Akamba Hall
came up

objectives

  1. Provide access to a wide range of books and learning resources
  2. Support academic excellence
  3. Encourage independent learning
  4. Foster a love for reading
  5. Promote information literacy skills
  6. Support curriculum development
  7. Provide a quiet study environment
  8. Promote reading habits
  9. Support academic research
  10. Provide access to quality resources
